Transportation

Flying In

For those flying to Minnesota, consider flying into Brainerd, MN (BRD). Delta offers a relatively affordable conenction between there and Minneapolis (MSP). We are happy to help coordinate a shuttle from the Brainerd airport to the venue and lodging locations.

Flights for BRD: DL 4273 or DL 4301

Amtrak

Chicago - Milwaukee - Madison* - St. Paul - Fargo - Minot

For anyone interested in traveling by rail, Amtrak's Borealis and Empire Builder routes would be your options. The Amtrak station nearest to our venue on the Empire Builder route is Staples, MN (SPL). Unfortunately, the departure/arrival times at this station are 2 and 5 AM. The times for the Borealis route are more convenient but the nearest station to the venue is Union Depot in St. Paul (MSP). Again, we would be happy to help coordinate car pooling for anyone intersted in these option.

*Amtrak does not stop in Madison unfortunately. The closest stations are Columbu (CBS) and Portage (POG).

Carpooling from Twin Cities

We're encouraging and happy to help coordinate carpooling from the Twin Cities area.
