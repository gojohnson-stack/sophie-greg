Transportation

Flying In

Those of you arriving by plane might consider flying into Brainerd, MN (BRD). Delta offers a relatively affordable flight to Brainerd from Minneapolis (MSP), and Brainerd is an easy 30-45 minute drive from our venue and nearby lodging options.

We are happy to help coordinate getting you from the Brainerd airport to the venue or your lodging location.

MSP to BRD Flights: DL 4273 or DL 4301

Amtrak

Chicago - Milwaukee - Madison* - St. Paul - Fargo - Minot

For anyone interested in traveling by rail, Amtrak's Borealis and Empire Builder routes would be your options. The Amtrak station nearest to our venue is Staples, MN (SPL) and is served by the Empire Builder. Unfortunately, the departure and arrival times at this station are 2 AM and 5 AM. The other option is the Borealis which has more convenient times but the nearest station to the venue is Union Depot in St. Paul (MSP). Again, we would be happy to help coordinate car pooling for anyone intersted in either of these options.

*Amtrak does not stop in Madison. The nearest stations are Columbus (CBS) and Portage (POG).
